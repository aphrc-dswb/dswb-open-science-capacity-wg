# Office Hours

```{note}
Office Hour calls in DSWB are modeled after the Coworking Calls in *The Turing Way*, but with a focus on enabling team members to ask questions and share challenges in order to get help from technical leads. You can learn more about different formats in [this chapter](https://book.the-turing-way.org/community-handbook/community-calls).
```

Pathfinder teams share their challenges ahead of time, an agenda is created, and the leads make preparations ahead of time. 

As we have grown, we have adjusted calls to better benefit our team members based on the phase of the project. It is a derivative of our [coworking calls](https://aphrc-dswb.github.io/dswb-open-science-capacity-wg/coworking-calls/), with a larger part of the session dedicated to the office hours. Other members of the team are welcome to join, as they could benefit from these sessions; however, should they choose to just work independently, breakout rooms can be created.

### Purpose of our Office Hour calls

Our office hour calls serve several key purposes for the DSWB community:

* **Challenge Clinics:** The Office Hours enable technical partners to troubleshoot technical challenges with Pathfinder teams.
* **Enhancing real-time interaction:** We often work in distributed teams and utilise meetings to share updates. Asynchronous communication, such as via email, WhatsApp, and Newsletters, is helpful but can sometimes be challenging if we want to get something done on time. Coworking calls provide a dedicated time for real-time interaction, problem-solving, and efficient project progress.
* **Providing Peer Support:** If you're facing an issue or need assistance with a particular task, these calls offer a supportive environment to seek input, share knowledge, and collaborate with trusted colleagues.

### Who is this Office Hour for

All DSWB members!
These calls are open to **all members of the Data Science Without Borders (DSWB) community**, regardless of their team or location.
If your team isn't presenting, you can simply show up for any amount of time during this hour.

### Call structure and size

There's no limit to the number of participants. We use breakout rooms within our virtual meeting platform - if needed - to accommodate both focused individual work and smaller group collaborations as needed during the call. Ideally, the technical partner addresses the challenge in the main room, and team members attend the call there too.
